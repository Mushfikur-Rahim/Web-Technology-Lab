Major Rivers of Bangladesh

Bangladesh is known as the land of rivers. Rivers play an important role in agriculture, transportation, culture and economy.

Overview

The major rivers of Bangladesh include Padma, Meghna, Jamuna, Surma and Karnaphuli. These rivers are essential for irrigation, fishing and transportation.

Major Rivers

Padma River

The Padma River is one of the largest rivers in Bangladesh.

Meghna River

Important for fishing and transportation.

Jamuna River

One of the widest rivers in Bangladesh.

Surma River

Flows through Sylhet region.

Karnaphuli River

Famous for hydroelectric power generation.
